The Divine Gift of Forgiveness

Neil L. Andersen

In this new book, Elder Andersen writes especially to those who are “awakening” unto God—those who are just beginning to discover or who seek the divine gifts and power of repentance and forgiveness in their lives. The Divine Gift of Forgiveness is organized in such a way that readers can choose the section or chapter that applies most to them and study it without having to have read from cover to cover. Leaders can similarly recommend specific chapters to those with whom they work.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main message of the book?

Forgiveness is a divine gift made possible through Jesus Christ’s Atonement.

Who is the book written for?

Members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and anyone seeking to understand repentance and forgiveness.

Does the book include real-life stories?

Yes, it includes personal stories, scriptural accounts, and real-life examples.

What role does Jesus Christ play in the book?

Jesus Christ is central, as forgiveness and repentance come through His Atonement.

How can this book help me spiritually?

It provides guidance on repentance, helping readers find peace and draw closer to Christ.
